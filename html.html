Top 90 HTML Interview Questions for Freshers & Experts

HTML stands for HyperText Markup Language. It is arguably the most widely used and popular programming language for web application development. Created in 191 by Berners-Lee but first published in 1995, HTML has evolved over the years and has seen multiple releases. HTML 4, published in 1999, was a breakthrough version in its evolution and gained a lot of popularity, seeing widespread adoption all over the globe. The latest and current version is HTML 5, which was published in 2012. This article will discuss the top 90 HTML Interview Questions you should know to crack those trying interviews and land your dream job.

HTML Interview Questions for Freshers

1. What is HTML?
